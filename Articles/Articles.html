Article 1: Meet Bella, Our Star Reader

Bella, a 3-year-old golden retriever, is one of our most successful literacy students. When she joined the program, Bella struggled to understand basic phonics. Thanks to her dedicated tutor, she now wags her tail with pride while reading Go, Dog, Go! aloud to her puppies.

Bella's owner shares, "I used to think she was barking for treats, but now I know she's practicing consonant blends. It's incredible!"
