How to Get Involved

  1. Donate

Your support helps us purchase:\n

  • Picture books with colorful illustrations.
  • "Scratch-and-sniff" educational materials.
  • Classic literature reimagined for dogs (e.g., Moby Bark and Romeo and Drooliet).

Ways to Donate:

  • Online: Visit www.pawsforliteracy.org/donate.
  • By Mail: Send checks to 123 Dogwood Lane, Woofington, USA.

  1. Volunteer

Become a literacy coach for dogs! Training sessions cover:

  • Recognizing barks that resemble vowels.
  • Using treats as motivational tools.

Sign up at www.pawsforliteracy.org/volunteer.
