About the Cause

In our community, many dogs are struggling with low literacy levels. From shelter pups yearning to read adoption ads to pampered pets who dream of reading Shakespeare, the need is greater than ever. Paws for Literacy works tirelessly to:

  1. Collect Donations: Every dollar buys books designed for early readers (chew-resistant, of course).
  2. Arrange Volunteer Tutors: Local dog lovers provide one-on-one phonics training and grammar lessons.
