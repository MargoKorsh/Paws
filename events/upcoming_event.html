Upcoming Event: "Bark & Browse Book Drive and Distribution"

Date : Saturday, March 23, 2025

Time : 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Location : Woofington Community Park, Pavilion 3

Hosted by: Paws for Literacy

Event Details

Join Paws for Literacy for our annual Bark & Browse Book Drive and Distribution Event ! This family-friendly day is dedicated to collecting books for local dogs in need while distributing educational materials to canine learners. Whether you're a long-time supporter or new to our mission, this is your chance to make a direct impact on improving canine literacy.

Schedule of Activities

Morning Session: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

  • Book Donations Drop-Off : Bring new or gently used books (early readers and picture books preferred) to the collection tent.
  • Dog Literacy Demonstrations : Watch live demos of volunteer tutors working with their canine students, showcasing phonics and reading skills.
  • Puppy Story Time : Enjoy a heartwarming story read by one of our trained volunteers, with plenty of wagging tails in attendance.

Afternoon Session: 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM

  • Book Distribution : Volunteers will distribute literacy kits to pre-registered families and shelters. Kits include books, scratch-and-sniff vocabulary cards, and phonics flashcards.
  • Volunteer Sign-Up Booth : Interested in becoming a literacy coach? Stop by the booth to learn more about how you can get involved or sign up on the spot!
  • Photo Booth : Capture the moment with your furry friend and a stack of their favorite books. Props and costumes provided!

Throughout the day, we'll have food trucks, dog-friendly snacks, and a live acoustic performance by local musician Rover Strum!

How You Can Help

We're counting on our amazing community to make this event a success! Here's how you can contribute:

  • Donate Books : New or gently used early-reader books, phonics guides, or children's classics are welcome.
  • Volunteer : We need helpers for donation sorting, event setup, and tutoring demonstrations. Sign up at www.pawsforliteracy.org/volunteer .
  • Spread the Word : Share the event with friends and family to help us reach more supporters!

Important Notes

  • Dog-Friendly Event : Well-behaved, leashed dogs are welcome to join the fun!
  • Parking : Free parking is available at the east lot of Woofington Community Park. Overflow parking will be directed to nearby lots.
  • Weather Contingency : In case of inclement weather, the event will move to the Woofington Civic Center, 123 Dogwood Lane. Updates will be posted on our website and social media channels.
